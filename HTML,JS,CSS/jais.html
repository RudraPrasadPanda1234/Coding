WELCOME TO OUR PORTAL!

Waste Reduction Portal is an online platform dedicated to empowering individuals, households, businesses, and communities to take meaningful steps towards minimizing food waste and fostering sustainable consumption practices. The food waste crisis has become a critical challenge, putting immense strain on our environment, economy, and society. To combat this issue and foster a more sustainable future,it introduces a Food Waste Reduction Portal (FWRP).
